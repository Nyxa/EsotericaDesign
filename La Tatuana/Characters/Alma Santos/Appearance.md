### Lacryma Dea
Her hair grows down to waist length, and is split down the middle in colour; the left side an inky black and dark royal purple, the other an iridescent white and blue. Her face is adorned with a small, white and blue mask covering her forehead, eyes and nose, with a black bar where her eyes are. Her lips and clothing are coloured like her hair, equally split down the middle. Her clothing is a dress in the same shapes and designs as the dresses of Aurora and Vespera, with the ends of the dress frilled and tattered. She wears no gloves or shoes in this form. Across her back, in colours matching the part of the dress they're on, are written the words '**Lacryma Dea**'. This form remains active for exactly 6 minutes and 42 seconds, though from Alma's (and the audience's) pov, it's just "much shorter than Angelus Mortem." Just like Angelus Mortem, Alma is completely restored when Lacryma Dea activates. 

Aurora is transformed into a new, white blade with a round hilt, and no guard, with golden trimmings along the blade, and '**Sanctus Lux**' inscribed on one side of the blade, in gold. The blade is a little over a half a meter long.

Vespera is transformed into what initially appears to be just a black and gold round hilt - however, when this hilt is placed within the shadows, a black and gold blade materializes, and can remain materialized for up to 4 seconds outside of the shadows. It has '**Sanctus Inanis**' inscribed in gold on the opposite side of Aurora's inscription. Additionally, she has a third blade of ice, **Osculumortis**, which she can materialize at will. Once materialized, it will completely freeze whatever living being it is inserted into, and shatter shortly thereafter.
