<h1><center>Nexus Awakening </center></h1>
***
**Nexus Awakening** (abbrev. **Awakening**) describes the progression of an entity's [[Hollow Nexus]], separating it into different *degrees* which describe the following traits and capabilities:
1. The entity's broadness of consciousness
2. The entity's ability to create [Injectors](Injector.md)
3. The entity's ability to harness, maintain, and transmute [[Qualia]]
4. The entity's resistance to **Qualia** effects
5. The entity's ability to travel between [[Ripples]]

>[!info]
>- The act of "raising" one's degree of **Awakening** is called "**Ascension**".
>- Every entity which has an [[Attunement]] will *also* have a degree of **Awakening**.
>- [[Primordial Remnants]] exist *outside* of the concept of **Awakening**, as they do *not* possess a **Hollow Nexus** or [[Fragment]].
>- Normal [[Remnants]] are effectively [Fetishes](Injector.md#Fetish) engraved onto the environment, which makes them **Inert**

The *tiers* all have their names described in the [[Elcheyed]] in [Nahvael](Nahvael.md); however, the [[Litchipedia]] lists them under the following names (**Nahvael** names in parentheses): 
- Inert (*Chelmud*)
	- **Inert** entities are entities which contain **Qualia**, but do *not* have a consciousness. 
	- Everyday objects fall into this category. 
	- **Fetishes** also fall into this category, despite how different they are from "dead matter" objects.
	- **Inert** entities are *not* capable of **Ascension**. This requires a **Hollow Fragment**, which they do not possess.
		- As such, this degree exists merely for completeness' sake; it is a part of the **Elcheyed**, which was written by [[Entreas]], who is [[well known]] for being needlessly detailed.
- Dreamer (*Nehar'tet*)
	- **Dreamer** entities are entities which *do* possess a consciousness, but are unaware of the existence of **Qualia**, and/or their ability to manipulate them. 
	- The vast majority of [[humans]] on [[Earth]] fall into this category.
	- **Dreamers** are capable of **Ascension**, as is every degree above them.
	- While they cannot manipulate **Qualia** consciously, their **Attunement** still has an impact on their tendencies and talents. For example, someone **Attuned** to [[Velocigen]] may be exceptionally nimble, or have a particularly intuitive understanding of spacetime.
		- However, a talent in itself isn't indicative of any particular kind of **Attunement**.
- Awakened (*Khamar'tet*)
	- **Awakened** entities are capable of manipulating **Qualia** to a minor extent.
	- These beings are the most common form of Qualia user.
	- They typically only create a single Injector, which they develop and maintain over their lifetimes.
	- For societies where Qualia are well-known and studied, most military power would come from Awakened soldiers; even higher-ranking ones.
	- They are more resilient and resistant to aging than Dreamers, tending to live slightly longer lives and being able to withstand more damage.
- Wanderer (*Kethsah*)
	- Wanderers are capable of traveling to other Ripples, although this does not come easy to them.
		- They can only travel into a Ripple, not out of it. This is referred to as One-Way Travel, and is a major shortcoming of Wanderers. 
	- Wanderers are few and far between on local scales, but in the grand scheme of things are fairly common.
		- In societies where Qualia use is common, the ratio of Wanderers to Qualia users is about 1 in 100,000
	- They are exponentially more powerful than the Awakened, and often create more than one Injector within their lifetimes.
	- They are more resilient, and more resistant to aging; tending to live at least twice as long as the Awakened of the same species.
	- The easiest way to become a Wanderer is to enter one's own Ripple -- something which is incredibly difficult for the average being to do. 
		- Due to Wanderers only being capable of One-Way Travel, they are discouraged from using this method without a Deva present who's willing to pull them out.
- Deva (*Kethra’at*)
	- Deva may travel into and out of other Ripples, known as Chain-link Travel. 
		- They can also do so with ease, as long as they can make contact with their destination's Nexus at least once.
	- Deva are exceedingly rare. In a universe with many Qualia-using societies, you're likely to only find a handful of Deva at best. 
		- Many universes have Qualia users without any Deva whatsoever.
	- They are notorious for having their entire bodies covered in Injectors.
		- They are the most prolific users of Fetishes, as they often run out of space on their own body to add Injectors.
		- In some rare cases, they instead have a small number of incredibly powerful Injectors, that they've honed over several decades or even centuries.
	- They are exponentially more resilient than Wanderers. 
		- Deva are biologically immortal, but not immune to damage.
		- In order for a Deva to take meaningful damage from Qualia energy, this usually takes the power of another Deva, or a dozen or so Wanderers.
	- They are capable of using multiple Injectors at the same time in order to combine their effects.
		- Doing so consumes considerably more Energy than using those Injectors separately. This amount is typically unsustainable for Wanderers, which is why it's mostly Devas that do this.
	- Deva are treated as demigods.
- Sephyr (*Nahva’at*)
	- Sephyr are effectively gods.
	- They are capable of traveling to any Ripple they've previously visited.
		- They can do so practically instantly, known as the Sephyr-Step.
	- You are likely to find about one Sephyr every few thousand Ripples or so, assuming you only count Ripples where Qualia are used by the population.
	- They typically do not possess many Injectors, or any Injectors at all.
		- Sephyr forego the need of Injectors. Their mastery over their Attunement is so great that they can produce effects on a whim.
		- Sephyr Injectors are typically holdovers from their journey to becoming a Sephyr. They typically keep them for sentimental value more than anything.
	- They are immortal.
		- A Sephyr can only be killed by being sealed away and tossed into a Ripple whose laws are so foreign that the Sephyr cannot exist within it. This effectively nullifies their existence, making it so they never existed in the first place, rather than killing them.
		- They can still be damaged by other Sephyr-level abilities. However, depending on their abilities, this may be hard to do even for other Sephyr.
	- They effectively have access to an infinite amount of Qualia Energy, as they draw their Qualia directly from their Nexus, rather than from the environment.
		- The Hollow Nexus is a connection to Nahla, and as such, a connection to the Void. The Void has no such thing as a quantity, so they can withdraw as much energy from it as they want.
	- Across all Nahla-verse stories, there are fewer than 10 Sephyr.
		- This number is still far larger than it normally would be, for how many Ripples the story covers.
		- The reason it's so high is because most of these Sephyr were personally trained by Entreas, who is a rare case of a being becoming a Sephyr of their own accord.
		- When a Sephyr trains a Deva, it is far more likely that they can achieve this level of power.
		- It is also for this reason that the stories have one Sephyr of each Attunement type. Entreas did this deliberately.
- Aun
	- A descriptor prefixed to any of the **Awakening** levels that indicates this entity is at this **Awakening** level in *two* types of **Qualia**
	- Aun are rare enough that even Nahla has seen fewer than a million of them, since the beginning of time, across a practically infinite number of Ripples.
	- The only two characters to have more than one Attunement in the story are Litchi and Akira.
	- An Aun Deva is roughly equivalent to a Sephyr in terms of raw power.
	- The only character to ever reach Aun Sephyr status is Akira, at the end of the entire saga.


- **Memorium**
	- The Qualia of **Information & Perception**
	- Governs the ability for things to possess qualities and traits, and for those traits to be perceived as distinct from one another.
	- Governs information transfer, which is a component of energy transfer.
	- Governs information persistence, in tandem with **Temporum**.
	- As an Attunement type, it is typically used for reconnaissance, illusions, the creation of imagery, and the alteration of an object's properties by imposing the user's imagination onto reality.

- **Temporum**
	- The Qualia of **Causality**
	- Governs the ability for things to have properties that can produce effects when interacting with other things with properties
	- Creates the **Temporal Field**, a field that stores all causal information of a Ripple
		- This can be understood as a field which stores a "back-up" of every "moment" that occurs within a Ripple
		- The Temporal Field is of a fixed size, so storing new information becomes slower as more information is added. 
			- The effect this has is that the passage of time within Ripples starts out incredibly fast relative to other Ripples, but slows down over the course of billions of years, until all Ripples eventually have a similar passage of time.
		- The Temporal Field is created by Temporum, but the information within it is comprised of Memorium.
	- Governs causal connections, which are a component of energy transfer.
	- As an **Attunement** type, it is typically used to observe the past, redefine causal events, and to amplify or diminish causal effects.
		- Despite its name, Temporum does not allow for time travel. Temporum allows the creation of "moments" and for one moment to produce the next moment. 
		- However, a "moment" within a Ripple comprises more elements than Temporum, as the **Temporal Field** is made of Temporum and Memorium, and the **Spacetime Field** is made of Temporum and **Velocigen**.
		- As Velocigen is what cycles the Spacetime Field to the next moment, an Attunement in both types is required to do anything remotely resembling time manipulation.
		- However, all Ripples operate on a single timeline. Branching is not possible, and the creation of a paradox induces a Schism, which undoes the paradox and creates a set of Primordial Twins.
		- In short, true time-travel is not possible in Nahla's multiverse.
	
- **Velocigen**
	- The qualia of **Space & Motion**
	- Governs the ability for things to have arbitrary positions in an arbitrary number of dimensions.
		- Creates the **Spacetime Field**, a field that contains information about the position and momentum of all objects within a Ripple
			- Position is stored as Velocigen information, and momentum as Temporum information. Thus, it is impossible to observe both at once, without possessing those two types of Attunement.
	- Governs the laws of motion by itself, and the laws of thermodynamics together with Temporum and **Oscillium**.
	- As an **Attunement** type, it is typically used to bend or warp space, adjust the flow of time, accelerate or decelerate objects, or to manipulate gravity.
	- Velocigen is widely considered to be the third most powerful type of Qualia.
	- Conversely, it's also the third hardest type to wield.

- **Oscillium**
	- The qualia of **Cycles & Spectra**
	- Governs the ability for things to have multiple valid states, and for them to gradually alternate between those states.
	- Introduces stability into a system by fusing together dichotomous concepts, and giving each of them equal agency.
	- Defines a **Spectrum** that interpolates between these dichotomous concepts.
	- Responsible for the existence of anything with a frequency, such as sound and light.
	- Governs other abstract cycles together with **Ethereon**, the most ubiquitous one being Life & Death.
	- As an **Attunement** type, it is typically used to manipulate anything with a frequency. In rare cases, it is used to manipulate more abstract cycles. Among the more unorthodox uses of Oscillium, Necromancy is fairly common.
	- Unlike all other Natural Qualia, Oscillium was not created as an independent type of Qualia, but rather as the fusion of Temporum and Velocigen.
		- As a result, any Aun Deva with an attunement in Temporum and Velocigen is indirectly also Wanderer level at manipulating Oscillium, making that combination of Attunements particularly dangerous.


- **Ethereon**
	- The qualia of **Concepts**
	- Creates the **Ethereal Vault**, a unique Field within a Ripple that contains information about every conceivable **Thing** this Ripple can contain.
	- Governs the ability for objects to be distinct, discrete Things.
	- Governs the ability for Things to combine to form new Things.
	- Defines the different levels of existence a Thing may exist within.
	- As an **Attunement** type, it is the most unique. 
		- As Ethereon governs concepts, it is incredibly hard to wield. This has led Ethereon users to formulate a different kind of strategy to making Injectors.
		- Rather than making Injectors directly, Ethereon users produce a **Projection**. This is a portion of their own selves which becomes an ingredient for the Injector.
		- As a result, **Projections** are a rare type of **Stigmata** that exists on the brain of the user.
		- Unlike other Injectors, **Projections** cannot have their effects decided by their creator. Instead, they represent a concept, and the creator may impose restrictions on this concept. The more intense the restriction, the more powerful the effect of this concept will be.
		- For particularly weak or beginner Ethereon users, they will also not have a choice in what the restriction will be.
		- Due to these quirks, Ethereon is said to be a "temperamental" type of Qualia, as it can produce unintended effects or require the user to do ridiculous things to utilize or activate their **Projection**.
	- A Sephyr in Ethereon is capable of perceiving the **Vault** itself.
	- The Ethereon Sephyr **Aelyn** stands out from her peers by being the only Sephyr who has as many Injectors on her body as a Deva. These are not visible though, as they all reside on her own brain.
		- This is because, by its nature, it's impossible to create an Ethereon Injector on the fly, even for a Sephyr. The Sephyr may not exceed the concepts within the **Vault**, and to create a Sephyr-level Injector with Ethereon, they must spend some time perusing the **Vault**.


- **Nahline**
	- The Qualia of **Structure & Growth**
	- Nahline is the essence of Nahla herself. It is the crystallized form of her Will, and a crucial component in the creation of Ripples.
	- It is the element that allows other types of Qualia to combine to form laws and things.
	- It acts as the protective barrier around each Ripple, slowing the effects of the **Whitevoid** that attempts to rip Ripples apart.
	- It serves as the primary component of the **Hollow Nexus**, and as such is a key ingredient in Ripple travel and Attunement.
	- It can convert to and from any type of Natural Qualia, but it is incompatible with **Nihilium**.
	- As an **Attunement**, it is typically used to create objects from nothing, and manipulate these objects. It can also be used to create homunculi, and accelerate the growth of organisms. Additionally, it has strong medicinal properties.
		- Nahline Devas are typically capable of producing Awakened-level effects of Natural Qualia types, and capable of combining them. This makes them somewhat similar to Aun, but combining these Injectors is inordinately difficult.
	- Aun in Nahline Attunement has never occurred in the history of the **Hollow Lattice**.


- **Nihilium**
	- The Qualia of **Decay & Creation**
	- Nihilium is the essence and crystallized form of the **Whitevoid**, and, to Nahla's dismay, as crucial to the existence of Ripples as Nahline is.
	- All Qualia, including Nahline, eventually decay to Nihilium.
	- It is a crucial component in creating the **Ethereal Vault**.
	- It is responsible for the expansion of Ripples.
	- It tears Ripples apart after they have died.
	- It destabilizes other Qualia types, causing them to behave in unexpected ways.
	- It can be converted into from any type of Qualia, but cannot be converted back to a specific type.
	- Introducing Nihilium into a system can send it into complete disorder.
	- Infecting a Kethra with Nihilium can ruin an entire Ripple.
	- When it comes into contact with an equal or lesser amount of Nahline, it transforms that Nahline into Nihilium.
	- When it comes into contact with a greater amount of Nahline, it transforms into **Blank Energy**, from which physical Natural Qualia Energy can be created.
		- Because of this property of Nihilium, it is essential to the birth of Ripples
	- As an **Attunement**, it is highly unpredictable. A skilled Nihilium user is capable of creating Injectors that narrow down the set of possibilities, and then learns to respond to each random outcome. 
		- An unskilled Nihilium user may well be seen as a natural disaster waiting to happen.
	- Because of its many negative effects, it has a bad reputation. On top of Attunements of it being rare, and usage of it being incredibly difficult; Nihilium users are typically seen as evil beings, or as living natural disasters. They are often killed before they can do any meaningful damage as a result.
