
<h1><center>Injector</center></h1>
***
### Stigmata
Injector applied to the body. Can only be of the wielder's Attuned type.

### Fetish
Injector applied to object. Wieldable by anyone, regardless of Attunement.

### Soulbrand
Genetically inherited Injector.

### Spectre
Injector on the eyes. Notoriously powerful, but also relatively rare. Not nearly as rare as Deva, though.

### Projection
A sub-category of **Stigmata** that contains partial markings in the *brain* of a subject. **Projections** are *exclusive* to [[Ethereon]], and *cannot* be created with any other type of **Qualia**.
