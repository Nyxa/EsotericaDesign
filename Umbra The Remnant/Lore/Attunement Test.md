<h1><center>Attunement Test</center></h1>
***
Performed by holding a piece of **charcoal** and channeling **Energy** into it. 

Students who are discovering their **Attunement** are told to channel enough Energy into their charcoal to ignite it. 

The way the charcoal reacts indicates not only what your Attunement is, but also which  **Polarity** you have an affinity for.


### Temporum (Causality)
The charcoal splits apart, and is suddenly red hot. Shortly after, it's back to its old state, unbroken and cool to the touch, like it never happened.


### Temporum (Interaction)
The charcoal immediately ignites into a massive flame, and burns to ashes in a matter of seconds.


### Velocigen (Space)
The charcoal heats up, then is compressed into a diamond. This diamond crumbles to dust once the student stops pouring Energy into it.


### Velocigen (Motion)
The charcoal vibrates and turns red hot, then pops out of the person's hand like a popcorn kernel, and bounces on its own for a few moments before stopping and cooling.


### Memorium (Information)
The charcoal becomes incredibly hot, burning everything around it, but it itself does not ignite or appear to be heated in any way. When the effect dissipates, the charcoal appears to be fine. However, upon contact, it immediately disintegrates to ashes.


### Memorium (Perception)
The charcoal catches flame immediately, and the flames are large and intimidating. However, it is not heated at all. When the fire dissipates, the charcoal is still intact, as if it never burned. Everything around it remains cool to the touch.


### Oscillium (Cycles)
The charcoal catches fire, and then the fire goes out. It then catches fire again, and goes out again. This persists as long as the student channels Energy into the charcoal.

### Oscillium (Spectra)
The charcoal gradually cools down, until ice starts forming on it. The more Energy the student pours into it, the colder it gets.

### Ethereon
The charcoal becomes the student's favourite thing, and maintains that form as long as Energy is being poured into it. When the student stops, it reverts back to being a charcoal. The ground underneath it will have been charred in the shape of the student's favourite thing.

### Nahline (Structure)
The charcoal heats into a purple glow, and rearranges itself into a lattice pattern similar to Bismuth.

### Nahline (Growth)
The charcoal burns purple for a few moments. It then becomes soft and wet to the touch, and starts bleeding.

### Nihilium (Decay)
A loud crackle can be heard, and the charcoal fizzles into particles that split apart from it, and disintegrate to nothing.

### Nihilium (Creation)
The charcoal is ignited into multi-coloured flames which quickly burn out, leaving behind the material that was burned to obtain it. In essence, if you burned red cedar to obtain the charcoal, it would burn to become red cedar. This effect leads to Nihilium sometimes being mistaken as a Temporum Attunement.
