A special type of skill reserved for those whose soul extends into their weapon. Utilizing the weapon energizes it with Qualia, which slightly improves its damage and speed (windup/recovery).

The maximum potency is reached when the weapon is fully charged, doubling these stat increases.

Upon being fully charged, the user can perform a Moxie Release; a powerful special attack which uses no Energy, but completely depletes the energy stored in a weapon.

Moxie may *only* be gained by performing Strikes or Perfect Parries; Normal Parry and Dodge do *not* raise Moxie, and Block *depletes* Moxie.
