
<h1><center><i>The Sunken City</i><br>Elphira</center></h1>
***

- The remains of a former [Esterian](Esteria.md) city that can be found underneath [[Malicis]]
- Generally soggy and fishy area
- Lots of broken down **Esterian** architecture, machines, and other constructions of theirs
- Home to the [[Agnosthesium]] 
	- A place that has begun to mirror various other nearby areas
	- The area where the [[Eiaran]] [[Kethra]], [Primeval Ophid Neketh](Elvira%20-%20Neketh%20~%20Primeval%20Ophid.md) resides.
	- The [[Vortex]] [Luscerna, the Ashen Echo](Vortex%20-%20Luscerna%20~%20Ashen%20Echo.md) can be found within the deepest reaches of the **Agnosthesium,** within the [[Hall of Reflections]].
