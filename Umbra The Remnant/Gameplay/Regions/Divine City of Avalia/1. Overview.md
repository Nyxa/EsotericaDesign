
<h1><center><i>The Divine City</i><br>Avalia</center></h1>
***

- A large city shaped like a birthday cake, where the highest "layer" houses religious figureheads that are also political leaders
	- The lower the layer, the lower the status of the inhabitants
	- Three main layer divisions:
		- [[Pristine Castle]]
		- [[Residential District]]
		- [[Slums]]
- Home to the most powerful military remaining in [[Eiare]]
	- This military is led by the fearsome [[Divine General]]
- Aggressively anti-[[Hybrid]]
	- Their religion forbids them from killing outside of war, so most Hybrids are relegated to the slums or prisons
- Residential Avalians worship the goddess [[Auris]]
	- They believe she is the physical incarnation of nature
	- They believe she granted herself birth to be able to fight off the embodiment of disorder, [[Mellivora]]
	- They believe [Injectors](Injector.md) are divine gifts from **Auris**
	- They believe [[Artifiction]] is a sin
	- They view [Esterians](Esteria.md) as evil
- Their architecture is intricate, resembling a mix of ancient Greek and Persian construction.
