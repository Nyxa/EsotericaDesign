## Overview
[Hakti](Hakti.md) is a *roaming* boss, who acts as a regular enemy until you can prove yourself to survive her opening attack. Should you get hit by her opener, then you will die a [Reform Death](Combat.md#Immortality). Should you survive it, then dying after that will result in a **Rewind Death**, just like with any other [[Anomaly]]. 

## Location
[Glistergrove](Glistergrove.md)


## Mechanics

##### Roaming
**Hakti's** roaming functions such that she has a *fixed set* of spawn locations, and, initially, can be found in *any* of these locations. Upon encountering her and dying to her **Opener**, she will abandon the location you've found her in, and will be encounterable in all spawn locations *except* that one. From that point onwards, she can always be found in all spawns except the previous one. 

Should you die to her in spawn location A, and then in location B, she will be encounterable in location A again; and B will now be deserted.

**Hakti** may be encountered from the very beginning of the game, though as far as balance is concerned, she is a late-game boss, intended to be fought starting from *Act 6*.

##### Opener
Upon being encountered, **Hakti** will have a certain response, based on how many times you've encountered her, and in what capacity. In all cases, there is a point where she will *attack* the player. This attack normally *kills in a single hit*, and thus must be mitigated; the exact gameplay mechanic that mitigates it *is still TBD*. The attack is called **Kestrel Dive**.

1. She will address your presence. Depending on whether you've interacted with [Luscerna](Luscerna.md) before, her reaction here may differ. The current list will assume you have *not* encountered **Luscerna** before fighting **Hakti**. In this case, she will warn you not to step closer, and if you do, she does her **Kestrel Dive** immediately.
2. She will recognise you by your scent, and admit it's odd that you've survived her attack. She was certain you had died last time.
3. She will start doubting whether you're a real person.
4. She asks what you want from her.
5. She starts becoming aggressive.


##### Weapon
**Hakti's** weapon consists of two emerald-tinted blades that are conjoined at their tips, and *may be detached at will* in order to *dual-wield* them. Dual-wielding *increases* her speed, but *lowers* her damage output. Regardless of whether she's dual-wielding or not, she can *always* throw the blade(s) like a boomerang.


##### Ghost
**Hakti** *never* parries, and will *always dodge* attacks instead. If she dodges outside of any recovery periods, she will attack [Umbra](Umbra.md) from the back. This attack can *only* be mitigated with a [Perfect Parry](Combat.md#Parry).
