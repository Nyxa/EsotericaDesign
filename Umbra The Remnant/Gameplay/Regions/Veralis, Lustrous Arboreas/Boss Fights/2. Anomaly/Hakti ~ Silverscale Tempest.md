## Overview
[Hakti](Hakti.md) is an *optional*, *late game* boss who is accessible from the very beginning of the game, and can be found roaming [Veralis](1.%20Veralis%20Overview.md). Her move-set is entirely oriented around *speed*, with an elemental theming of *wind* and *electricity*.

## Mechanics

##### Roaming
**Hakti** is a *roaming* boss, who acts as a regular enemy until you can prove yourself to survive her opening attack. Should you get hit by her opener, then you will die a [Reform Death](Combat.md#Immortality). Should you survive it, then the actual fight begins; dying after that will result in a [Rewind Death](Combat.md#Rewind), just like with any other [[Anomaly]]. 

**Hakti's** roaming functions such that she has a *fixed set* of spawn locations, and, initially, can be found in *any* of these locations. Upon encountering her and dying to her **Opener**, she will abandon the location you've found her in, and will be encounterable in all spawn locations *except* that one. From that point onwards, she can always be found in all spawns except the previous one. 

Should you die to her in spawn location A, and then in location B, she will be encounterable in location A again; and B will now be deserted.

While **Hakti** may be encountered from the very beginning of the game, she is intended to be fought starting from *Act 6*, as far as balance is concerned. With enough skill and dedication, a player could still defeat her in the early game.

##### Opener
Upon being encountered, **Hakti** will have a certain response, based on how many times you've encountered her, and in what capacity. In all cases, there is a point where she will *attack* the player. This attack normally *kills in a single hit*, and thus must be mitigated.

1. She will address your presence. Depending on whether you've interacted with [Luscerna](Luscerna.md) before, her reaction here may differ. The current list will assume you have *not* encountered **Luscerna** before fighting **Hakti**. In this case, she will warn you not to step closer, and if you do, she does her **Kestrel Dive** immediately.
2. She will recognise you by your scent, and admit it's odd that you've survived her attack. She was certain you had died last time.
3. She will start doubting whether you're a real person.
4. She asks what you want from her.
5. She starts becoming aggressive.


##### Weapon
**Hakti's** weapon consists of two emerald-tinted blades that are conjoined at their tips, and *may be detached at will* in order to *dual-wield* them. Dual-wielding *increases* her speed, but *lowers* her damage output. Regardless of whether she's dual-wielding or not, she can *always* throw the blade(s) like a boomerang.


##### Swift Gale
**Hakti** *never* parries, and will *always dodge* attacks instead. If she dodges outside of any recovery periods, she will attack [Umbra](Umbra.md) from the back. This attack can *only* be mitigated with a [Perfect Parry](Combat.md#Parry).

### Injectors

- **Silverscale Tempest**
	- **Hakti**'s weapons have a special kind of [Moxie](Combat.md#Moxie).
		- With each successive *melee* attack, **Hakti**'s weapons become increasingly *electrified*, and the cooldowns between her attacks *decrease*.
		- **Moxie Saturation**: **Hakti** performs 3 rapid **Strikes**, then initiates a **Release**.
		- **Moxie Release**: **Hakti** temporarily gains the ability to move extremely quickly, making it appear as though a clone of hers appears to attack alongside her. However, these clones are just **Hakti** herself, and attacking either will damage her.
	- Unlike most uses of **Moxie**, **Hakti** can continue to accrue it even whilst her **Release** is active, and she may activate **Release** *again* regardless of any previously activated **Releases**.
	- This effect compounds—her second **Release** will activate much faster than the first because she is attacking more frequently; however, at 3(?) copies of herself, she will start *scorching the ground* underneath her, and will rapidly wear out her body and start taking damage.
	- After taking a bit of damage, **Hakti** will retreat and attempt to fight from mid-range instead.
- **Kestrel's Reaping Dive**
	- **Hakti** jumps up high in the air, then swoops down with extreme speed, *cleaving* through anything in her path.
	- She repeats this, with each cleave happening *faster* than the last, and different cleaves requiring different responses to be mitigated.
	- If **Umbra** is hit by this attack, she will experience a **Reform Death**.
- **Divine Virtues of the Sky Serpent**
	- **Orbital Fangs**
		- **Hakti** may throw out either of her blades, whipping up a *small tornado* around them as they boomerang back to her.
		- If she retains a blade, she may *continue attacking* with just that blade, even while the tornado is out.
		- Without blades, she *loses access to melee attacks*.
	- **Silver Sprite
		- **Hakti** may fire peculiar looking lightning bolts from her fingertips. This has a *long cooldown*.
		- The lightning bolts look like silvery, somewhat fleshy versions of *Red Sprites*.![](Pasted%20image%2020250714053735.png)
		- They expand outwards relatively slowly for a lightning bolt—almost like a growing snowflake—before fading away.
		- This attack deals *heavy damage*.
	- **Wind God Palm**
		- **Hakti** may direct the flow of air to create strong currents in certain directions.
		- These currents can be large, which restricts movement in a certain area.
		- They may also be small, functioning like wind projectiles.
