### Naming
Altireans don't view family as those who birthed you by blood per se (some bloodline shenanigans exist, like in [[Kayanna Litchi]]'s family). The ones who *name* you are considered family, this includes:
	- Your "real" name
	- nicknames
	- any given name
	- any chosen name

Giving a nickname in Altire is a sign of friendship, bonding, or even relationship, and is generally considered a big deal. See: [Umbra](Umbra.md) becoming a part of [[Kayanna Litchi]]'s family via being named by Litchi
