### Musical Motif
**Umbra**'s musical theme has the unique property that it initially *does not resolve*. It starts out *incomplete*.

As the player progresses through the game and resolves key storylines that develop **Umbra**'s character, her theme will gain additional segments and notes, until the [[True Ending]] is reached. The credits to the **True Ending** contain **Umbra**'s *complete* musical motif.
