### Titles
[[Squall]] Title: *Demon*
Boss Title: *The Demon of Hiraizumi*

### Details
Awakening: [Aun Deva](Awakening.md)

### Injectors
- [Benzaiten](Akira%20~%20The%20Demon%20of%20Hiraizumi.md#Benzaiten 「辯才天」)
	- Temporum
	- An Injector that Akira uses to perform the [[Dance of the Flowing Lily]] technique, a gradually evolving combat form with seven stages. 
	- At the core of each stage lies the true ability of Benzaiten: Akira can shrink and extend the causality of herself and objects around her, giving her opponents very little time to react to her attacks without requiring her to actually move quickly.
- **Takemikazuchi**
	- Temporum
	- Another Injector used with **Dance of the Flowing Lily**
	- Allows Akira to manipulate the state of matter she has infused with her Energy. 
	- Notably, the amount of Energy required to turn air (specifically gaseous oxygen mixed with carbon dioxide) into a liquid is too great, and will discharge in the form of lightning before it can be properly transformed. This is why she named it after the lightning deity.
- **Ayakashikone**
	- Aun Injector.
	- Also used with the **Dance of the Flowing Lily**
	- Akira creates a clone of herself in the past, which performs a different action from hers up to the present. 
	- This lets her, as an example, perform a slash from multiple angles simultaneously, or cut someone without ever moving.
- **Toyotama-hime**
	- Velocigen
	- The final Injector used with **Dance of the Flowing Lily**, which allows Akira to control the shape and motion of liquids she has infused with her Energy.
- **Amaterasu**
	- Velocigen
	- Akira shines with radiant light, blinding her opponents.
		- She achieves this by simply emitting Velocigen energy evenly around herself, giving her a golden glow, much like the goddess this Injector is named after.
	- The light also emits heat, though it is not like surrounding herself with flame.
		- If used in combination with **Ikugui**, this does not apply, and she can amp the heat to well over 100C.

- **Shidai Ten'nō, Yotsuori no Mon 「四大天王四つ折りの門」**
	- Aun Injector
	- Translates to: *Four Heavenly Kings, the Quadrifold Gates* 
	- Comprised of four different "Realms" that trap opponents in environments with specific effects.
	- These effects are all combinations of abilities of enemies Akira has faced in the past.
	- Each Realm is named after a Heavenly King:
		- He Whose Ears Discern All Lies, *Bishamonten* 
			- There is no light. It is raining. Any who dare speak shall drown in their spit.
		- He Whose Winds Bring Forth All Roots, *Zōjōten* 
			- The sky is blue. Winds periodically blow through the realm. When they do, one must stand still, or they will have their feet eaten by the ground, and slowly turn into a plant.
		- He Whose Strings Uphold The Realm, *Jikokuten* 
			- Music plays in the realm. One must continuously move. Those that cease moving will go insane, and continuously hallucinate being eaten limb-first by a giant white Buddha, until they die of stress.
		- He Whose Gaze Sees Through The Veil, *Kōmokuten*
			- The sky is red. All sound is gone. There is a yellow eye in the sky, shining a beam down unto the ground. Those caught within the beam are immersed in Corvesse's **Flames of Penance**, until they die of sleep deprivation.

- **Susanoo-no-Mikoto**
	- Temporum
	- Akira may create causal magnifications of her strikes. This typically manifests as a giant, disembodied version of the striking limb appearing and replicating the strike at an area of her choosing.
- **Ame-no-Uzume**
	- Aun Injector
	- Akira rapidly jumps between a moment in the past, and a moment in the future, rendering her invisible in the present. 
	- Someone who looks directly at where she is will still get a sense of Deja-vu, like they just saw someone there. This is because they technically did.

- **Kagutsuchi**
	- Velocigen
	- Akira amplifies her raw physical speed, scaling with how much Energy she expends on it.
	- Because it is an amp to physical speed, it puts a strain on her body, and induces heat. 
		- Exceeding sonic speeds causes a boom.
		- Because she's going faster, her strikes are more powerful.
		- Going fast enough may cause parts of her body to ignite.
	- Kagutsuchi is the reason she lost half her body, when she approached the speed of light and caused a massive explosion.
	- This Injector cannot safely be used without **Ikugui**.
- **Ikugui**
	- Temporum
	- Akira manipulates the causal relationship between herself, her Injectors, and their effects.
	- Effectively, this prevents her from being damaged by her own abilities.
	- This Injector only affects herself.
	- At times, this Injector may lead to manipulation of the flow of time within her body, particularly when it interacts with a **Velocigen** Injector such as **Kagutsuchi**.
		- Despite this, Ikugui is a **Temporum** Injector and not Aun. Any time manipulation is a coincidental result of her different abilities interacting.
- **Izanami**
	- Aun Injector. 
	- Creates a **Temporal Region**, which may have any of the following properties, chosen by Akira at the time of its creation:
		- **Rejector**: Anything that happens within this Space, did not occur.
			- This includes the passage of time.
		- **Projector**: Replicates a sequence of Temporal data. 
			- It is easy to conceive of this as a VR video of a sequence of events, that can be reconstructed with Temporum energy extracted from any object nearby those events.
		- **Hypotime**: Time flows faster than the surrounding area. 
		- **Hypospace**: A region of space without a size, but with a **Causal Record**.
	- The Space is not restricted to individual properties, and may hold all properties at once; though this is extremely taxing on Akira.
	- The use cases are as follows:
		- The **Rejector** is useful for training the Hollow Nexus, or for interrogations. Akira is the only person who can remember what occurs within a **Rejector Region** that she created, which can allow her to extract information from people without them technically ever having given her that information, let alone remembering it.
			- Akira uses this to Awaken Alma, something that takes decades normally. Because Awakening exclusively involves the Hollow Nexus, and the Nexus exists outside of time, this is one of the rare things that persist outside of a **Rejector Region**.
		- The **Projector** is useful for viewing the past. Acquiring the data necessary is extremely difficult, but when done properly, it allows one to examine the scene in its entirety.
		- **Hypotime** may seem useful at first, as it lets you save time relative to your surroundings. However, it does not prevent aging. Thus, it is only useful to characters that are immune to aging.
			- At maximum power, a **Hypotime Space** can run up to ten times faster than its surroundings.
		- **Hypospace** is an area of space which technically does not exist. By lacking a size, it cannot contain things. However the presence of a **Causal Record** means things occurred within it.
			- **Hypospace Regions** are immune to sensory abilities. They cannot have Temporal data extracted from them, and cannot be viewed remotely. They are completely isolated from everything else.
				- Akira also refers to this as her **Pocket Void**.
			- The presence of a Causal Record allows people to enter and exit the space, and do things within the space, despite the space technically not existing.
				- This is not considered a paradox as it's abusing a peculiarity of how spatial espionage works, rather than performing a logically inconsistent action.
			- People can see each other and breathe within this space. This is entirely a feature added in intentionally by Akira, and pockets of oxygen randomly vanishing in an area is the only way to tell the presence of a **Hypospace Region**.
			- Akira uses this in Altire, to discuss plans for war with the Celestials without Entreas being able to use Cyrell's All-Seeing Eye to spy on them.

- **Ame-no-Tokotachi**
	- Temporum
	- Akira reverts causality in her body, completely undoing any damage done to her. 
	- The cost of this ability lies not in the amount of damage that she repairs, but the recency of the damage. 
		- The further back in the past the damage occurred, the greater the Energy cost. 
	- Akira must be alive and conscious to activate this Injector. However, if she is, she can undo even losing the bottom half of her body.
	- A secondary cost is in the aging of her cells, though this becomes a non-issue once she becomes a Deva. 
	- The repair takes time, and is not instant.
	- The energy cost, even for recent damage, is so great that even in an Aun Deva state, she cannot use this Injector more than a handful of times within a battle.
		- Becoming a Temporum Sephyr allows her to maintain this ability indefinitely.
