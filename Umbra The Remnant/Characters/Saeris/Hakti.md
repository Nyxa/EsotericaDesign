## Appearance
**Hakti** is a very skinny, almost frail looking girl. She wears a black hakama, tied down at her ankles by bandages, and has her chest covered by several bandages as well. She has very short, black hair, and wears a very long and thin strip of cloth around her eyes, tied around the back of her head. The cloth has dark brown stains around where her eyes sit. Additionally, she has a prominent branding scar on her forehead.
