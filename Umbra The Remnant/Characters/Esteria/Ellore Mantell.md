### Life in Esteria
**Ellore** was an [Esterian](Esteria.md) *Clockwork Technician* who specialized in *Mechanized Rail* for **Esterian** infrastructure. 

She was fascinated by clockwork technology, ever since her parents gave her a *pocket watch* when she was a child and she took it apart to figure out how it worked, only to be sad that it was no longer functioning. She took it upon herself to learn how to put it back together, and this started her journey of becoming a clockwork technician.

She eventually got a job at **Estrail United** where she primarily repaired *Clockwork Wagons* and railways.




### Fate of the Last Wave
After the members of the [[Last Wave Exiles]] decided to split into 2 groups where one would explore beneath the surface, and the other would remain above as lookouts and for the safety of the delving team, the **Annex** seated deep within [Elphira](Gameplay/Regions/Elphira,%20the%20Sunken%20City/1.%20Overview.md) opened and released a massive blast of [Nihilium](Qualia.md#Nihilium), completely scrambling the delving team, leaving them as disfigured, immortal **Meta-Remnants**.

**Ellore**, having heard a strange noise coming from the *crater* leading into **Elphira**, happened to be staring into it when the blast hit, and it scrambled her face—turning it into a *pocket watch*—and overloaded her with [Qualia Energy](Qualia.md). This overflow led to her completely enveloping the area in a **Memorium**-infused miasma, which warps and distorts everything within it.

Many of the remaining **Exiles** on the surface died from this event. The others became warped themselves, some turning into enemies **Umbra** would later encounter, such as [Lemea](Malicis%20-%20Lemea%20~%20Blighted%20Lamb%20of%20the%20Sands.md) and [HAK-TOH-EKK](Malicis%20-%20HAK-TOH-EKK%20of%20the%20Faces.md).

The **Exiles** who were in **Elphira** at the time of the blast ended up roaming the underground, having completely lost their sanity and sense of self. The scout of the **Last Wave**, **Luscerna**, managed to avoid the blast; but the quaking of the earth around her led to her falling deep into the depths of **Elphira**, the **Agnosthesium**, where the high volumes of **Nihilium** eventually claimed her to become a **Meta-Remnant** as well.
