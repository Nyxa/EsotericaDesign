A young girl from the [[Maylith]] clan that is initially found carrying a chipped off piece of the [Great Maylith Seal](Great%20Maylith%20Seal), and wielding it as a makeshift "blade." She challenges [Umbra](Umbra.md) to a "fight," but is clearly not equipped for mortal combat. Umbra thus decides not to hit the girl, and instead dodge and block her until she tuckers out.

Garnet will then invite Umbra to "come visit her sometime" and dart off, though she never tells Umbra where to visit her. She can later be found in [Amberveil](Amberveil.md).
