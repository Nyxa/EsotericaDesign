***
**Touch of the Nullgazer** is a planned **expansion** for the **Umbra: the Remnant** base game.

It features three new regions, two of them being within the mysterious [[Calmerra]] that is mentioned in the lore so often in the base game.

In **Touch of the Nullgazer**, [Umbra](Umbra.md) is met with an invitation from a servant of [[Entreas]] himself; [[Estrella Vachsy]], a [[Deva]] who was sent to [[Eiare]] to gather the resources that were being generated as a result of **Eiare** being attached to two [[Primordial Ripples]].

**Estrella** needs **Umbra** to help her out, because she messed up her duties and accidentally spawned two [[Nullgazers]]; [[Halgeth]] and [[Astrophe]]. These **Nullgazers** have severely distorted the land they occupied, and **Estrella** was forced to contain them within a [[barrier]]. 

The first two areas of this DLC have different mechanics to the base game; they function more similarly to a *Roguelike* than anything else. The third area, however, returns to the old format.

The first two areas follow a format where **Umbra**'s upgrades from the base game have no effect. Instead, she may only use upgrades acquired in the respective **Nullgazer** areas; this means the **Godsmeat Grove** upgrades do not work in the **Carousel of Nothings**, and vice versa—with the exception of upgrades that work in the overworld, but not within these areas.

As a result, the upgrades **Umbra** gets here are far more flexible and powerful than upgrades from the overworld. 

[[Rewinds]] still work, however they will rearrange the layout of the **Nullgazer** area they were used in. 

This may be mitigated with **Rewind Protectors**, but those are limited use. Some of them persist between runs, whilst others may only be used within a run.

Each **Nullgazer** has their own area:

### Terragorger Halgeth ~ Godsmeat Grove
The **Godsmeat Grove** is a forest where all the vegetation has numerous eyes that observe you wherever you go.

Most of Godsmeat Grove remains undeveloped; however, the bossfight with **Halgeth** has a few key points:

- Initially, it has an appearance similar to popular depictions of a **Wendigo**, except with eyes all over its fleshy skin.
- It will then transform into something more resembling a biblically accurate **Cherub**.
- Once that dies, the surrounding forest closes its eyes, and the vegetation itself becomes "more of **Halgeth**" - revealing that this entire forest *is* **Halgeth**.
- The rest of the bossfight involves traversing the **Grove** whilst avoiding the boss, whilst finding its various "hearts" strewn across the forest that are now active.
- Destroying all of its hearts leads to a final phase with a gigantic flesh-plant creature.


### Starweaver Astrophe ~ Carousel of Nothings 
The **Carousel of Nothings** is a mirage-like land. We've still yet to determine how it differs from [Malicis-Miasma](Gameplay/Regions/Taboo%20Isles%20of%20Malicis/1.%20Overview.md).

The relevant boss, **Astrophe**, is found initially as a large *egg*. After some requirement has been met, this egg will hatch.

This fight is existential and takes us into several unsettling voids.


### Estrella Vachsy, Left Hand of the Celestial Kaiser
This fight takes place in the [[Empty Shrine]], and serves as the bridge between the base game and the [[Celestials]], who are far more prominent in the planned [[Altire]] sequel.
