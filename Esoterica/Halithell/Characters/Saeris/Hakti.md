## Appearance
**Hakti** is a very skinny, almost frail looking girl. She wears a black hakama, tied down at her ankles by bandages, and has her chest covered by several bandages as well. She has very short, black hair, and wears a very long and thin strip of cloth around her eyes, tied around the back of her head. The cloth has dark brown stains around where her eyes sit. Additionally, she has a prominent heretic branding scar on her forehead.

## Story
**Hakti** is the *last remaining survivor* of the [Saeris clan](Saeris.md).

Much like her clanmates, **Hakti** is a friendly and gentle soul at heart. She was dedicated to her *martial arts* practice and *fishing*, which she used to contribute to the village of [Ashenhearth](Ashenhearth.md). She worked hard and cared a lot about her clan, and was on good terms with everyone else, *despite being quite clumsy*.

While she was well-liked by her community, she *struggled to form any deeper connections* with anyone other than her neighbour and best friend, [Luscerna](Luscerna.md). 

"**Luci**," unlike **Hakti**, was very popular in **Ashenhearth**. She was *witty*, *kind*, and *energetic*; always able to *adapt* to new situations, and very good at getting herself out of trouble; something her *curious* and *carefree* nature would regularly get her into.

Despite their differences, **Luci** was *extremely fond* of **Hakti**.
