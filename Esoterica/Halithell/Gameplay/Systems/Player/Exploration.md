***
This document contains all exploration-specific systems available to the player. It does *not* take into account progression order, and instead details the *total* amount of combat systems that will be available throughout the game.


### Alliciency
[Umbra](Umbra.md) automatically absorbs [Qualia](Qualia.md) from the environment, as well as from naturally interacting with enemies and allies alike. Her absorption does *not* affect the energy levels of objects or entities in any noticeable way.

> [!note]
It would be cool if, as you progress through the game, your **Qualia** gauge starts rising automatically, to signify she is draining the environment around her. It's not explained anywhere in the game; she just has a secret passive ability to absorb energy from around her which grows more potent as she grows more powerful (Aka defeating **Anomaly**+ bosses, or finding **Dahlias**).


### Black Dahlia
The **Black Dahlia** is a special kind of [[Nahline]] flower that will react strongly to **Umbra**, rapidly growing large when she interacts with it, and serving as a bed where she can replenish her stats, and reform on death.

>[!info]
>The [[Dahliantis]] is a special type of praying mantis that assumes the form of a **Black Dahlia**. They only exist within [[Eiare]] due to the influence of [[Orthurys]].


### Qualia Droplets
**Qualia Droplets** are condensed units of **Physical Qualia** which may be collected by **Umbra**. They can be found in the world, obtained by defeating enemies, or from alternative sources (?). **Umbra** has individual **Droplet Containers** for each type of **Qualia**, except **Nihilium**, which she is *not* compatible with.
