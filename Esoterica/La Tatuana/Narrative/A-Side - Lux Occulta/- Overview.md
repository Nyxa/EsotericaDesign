
<center><h1>La Tatuana</h1><h4>Lux Occulta</h4></center>
***
**Lux Occulta** is the first half of the La Tatuana story. It follows **Alma Santos** as she tries to establish herself as a force of nature that goes after criminals, and **Havushe Neziri**, the genius Interpol detective who is enlisted to hunt after her.

Alma, having been traumatized at youth by watching her best and only friend **Mikayla Stratos** be murdered before her very eyes, is given a second chance when she meets a mysterious masked lady, who introduces herself as **Akira**.

Akira teaches Alma that underneath the physics of the world lies an intricate system that can be controlled through consciousness. Through this, Alma develops several abilities, one of which she uses to very convincingly fake her death.

Alma has two primary goals.

First, she wants revenge for her best friend. Initially, she just wants to take out the two guys responsible, but after a very bloody search for the culprits, she finds out that they are already dead. In the midst of her frustration, she is offered a job to work as an assassin 

Second, she wants to find her mother Ixchel, who is an extremely low profile criminal who leads the Yaxche Syndicate; headquartered in Totonicapán, Guatemala. Alma knows nothing about her mother; only her first name.





Havushe wants to catch Alma and is afforded a level of resources no Interpol agent prior to her has ever been granted.
