
<center><h1>La Tatuana</h1><h4>Timeline</h4></center>
***
###### December 20, 1982
**Akira is born**.

###### July 27, 1994
Akira travels to Solipsis for the first time.

###### Early 1995
Ixchel meets Humberto

###### October 31, 1997
**Alma is born**.

###### September 3, 2000
Alma's grandfather is murdered.

###### March 2003
Alma moves to the UK with her father.

###### May 7, 2010
**Mikayla is murdered**.

###### September 2013
Orthurys and Sallerys attach to Eiare.

###### June 6, 2015
Litchi appears in England.

###### March 2017
Litchi is caught on Earth, and accidentally escapes to Eiare.

###### April 5, 2018
Akira returns to Earth to watch over Umbra, as per Nahla's instructions.

###### April 12, 2018
Akira takes an interest in Alma.

###### May 7, 2018
Akira resolves to help Alma.

###### May 13, 2018
Akira attempts to talk to Alma for the first time, to no avail.

###### July 17, 2018
Alma accepts and believes Akira. 
Alma visits Eiare for the first time.
Akira is currently 35 years old, and Alma is 21.

###### November 15, 2018
Akira starts training Alma.

###### January 2019
Alma creates her first Injector, Hand of Serenity.

###### March 2019
Alma creates her second Injector, Hand of Antithesis.
Alma decides to start taking training more seriously.

###### April 24, 2019
Alma fakes her death using **Vespera: Shadow Double**.

###### April 27, 2019
Alma's Shadow Double is buried.
Alma starts living with Akira, in Solipsis.

###### September, 2020
Humberto tries to depart from the UK to go looking for Ixchel, using money he's spent the past week saving up.

He is unable to travel by normal means due to COVID restrictions, and so he resorts to alternative methods.

This gets him in trouble, though he survives.

###### August 15, 2023
Alma returns from training with Akira.
Alma is currently 25 years old. Akira is 40.
