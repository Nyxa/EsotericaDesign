
<h1><center>Saeris Clan</center></h1>
___
The **Saeris Clan** consists of a mixture of *former* [Maylith](Maylith.md) and [Niandes](Niandes%20Clan.md) clan members who decided to band together in an effort to achieve a form of *truce*. They strive for a *pacifist lifestyle*, and they worship the [[Silverscale Sky Serpent]]; a *swan* with the neck and head of a *snake*.

Despite their *pacifist lifestyle*, they are still very well versed in the *martial arts*, with their own *unique techniques* crafted by fusing the heavy and aggressive **Maylith** style with insightful and patient **Niandes** spiritualism. 

Their primary motto around combat is:

*"A clan of peace must always be ready to go to war, to defend its peace."* 

Therefore, *they view combat as a last resort* that one must be prepared for. They believe in resolving disputes *diplomatically*, through conversation, patience, and listening to the other side.

The **Saeris** have a *hierarchy of responsibility*; they view each other as being of equal importance, but those who are capable of shouldering greater responsibilities are typically in charge of those who can't shoulder as many as them. 

They are *very hospitable* to outsiders, even welcome outsiders into their clan; with the caveat that it is a one-time endeavour. Joining them is seen as a *life-long commitment*, and they *do not welcome deserters*. Additionally, you must still undergo a **Trial of Dedication** to prove that you are serious about joining.

The **Trial of Dedication** is not something restricted to outsiders, either. For descendants of **Saeris** members, this **Trial** serves as their *rite of passage* when they finally *come of age*. This means that **Saeris** members are always in the clan *by choice*. No memberships are handed out by birthright.

Once inaugurated into the clan, its **Silverscale** symbol would be *tattooed upon their forehead* in a *henna-like tint*; with slight alterations depending on their specialty of expertise, which in turn placed them somewhere within their *hierarchy*.

The **Saeris** are located within [Ashenhearth](Ashenhearth.md), without any other villages, as they have only existed for a few generations.
